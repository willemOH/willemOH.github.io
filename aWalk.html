  • A Walk Having a passion for mindfulness and believing that current mindfulness and meditation VR experiences were not taking full advantages of what VR has to offer, I prototyped one as part of my Senior Work at Bennington College. Developed in Unity with C# and modeled in Blender.
  • Breathe Naturally When landed, butterflies flapt their wings to a rhythm inspired by the yogic breathing techniques of Pranayama. Scientifically speaking, following the wing flaps with your breath brings your breathing rate to 4 breaths per minute with twice as much exhalation time as inhalation, making you calmer and more relaxed.
  • A Mind of their Own Here you can see the dynamic pathfinding engine at work to give each butterfly its own unique flight pattern that responds to its environment.
