  • Activism in AR These AR filters were created in Spark AR for Alchemy Immersive in an effort to use social media AR filters for environmental activism.
    Scroll down for individual filters.
  • "Daily Green Pledge" In the style of "which _____ are you", this AR filter randomly commits you to one environmentally friendly action for that day. In posting and sharing this action, your accountability for it increases. Created with Spark AR Studio, Blender, and Photoshop.
  • "Help Austrailia Fires" Created during the peak of the 2020 Austrailian Wildfires, this AR filter transforms your surroundings and puts you into the midst of the terrible fires. A rotating board disseminates information about where and how you can help the ongoing efforts.
  • "Caught in Plastic" A test run for creating AR filters in Spark AR, this filter puts you in a cinching situation of ocean life that is impacted by our waste.
